Racing Sim

This is an racing sim that I made with a group for a deep dive week in school. We got the task to make a driving sim in a timespan of 5 days. My biggest problem was that I didn't really know how to make a race game which shortened my time because I needed to do some research. As soon as we did our research we talked about what we wanted to make and just started working on the game. Everything was going great and we were able to show a working sim with gas, break, shifting, checkpoints and respawns.
