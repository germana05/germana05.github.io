Head Soccer Remake

Head soccer was a game I played a lot when I was younger and lately I thaught what if I use my knowledge to try and remake that game. The game is fully focused on a 1v1 soccer game. Every game only lasts a minute. During the game you will try to score by kicking the ball in the opponents goals. While playing your power slowly charges when the power bar is full you can activate it and as soon as you touch the ball your power shot activates. You can choose between different character and every character has his own power. By playing games you earn coins which you can use to buy upgrades for you character of to buy hats for your character.
